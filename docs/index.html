How to solve it

To solve the puzzle you must move the pieces into the empty space, sliding them until reach the right place. Once each piece is settled in the correct order, the puzzle is completed and you will pass to the next level. When the puzzle is solved, You'll score will be saved in a ranking with your results and those of other users.
